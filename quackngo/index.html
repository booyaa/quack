Usage

This crate is on crates.io and can be used by adding quack to the dependencies in your project's Cargo.toml.

[dependencies]
quackngo = "0.1"

and this to your crate root:

extern crate quackngo;
use quackngo::Quack;
println!("{:#?}", Quack::new("!imdb+rushmore"));
